How to play

The aim of minesweeper is to discover all the free spaces without detonating the mines.
The number that appears on the boxes clicked indicates the number of mines in the vicinity: left or right, top or bottom, or diagonally
Using the indications given by these figures, you can know where the mines are.
If a box indicates "1" and there is only one undiscovered box next to it, then a mine is hiding there!

One click = free a box.
Double click = place/remove a flag.
